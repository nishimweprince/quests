Welcome to the official YYUSSA Group blog. We are thrilled to launch this platform where we will share insights, updates, and expertise from our three core business divisions: real estate, import & export, and logistics.

Over the past 30 years, YYUSSA Group has grown from a local enterprise into a multi-sector conglomerate serving clients across East and Central Africa. This blog is our way of connecting with partners, clients, and the broader business community — sharing what we have learned and where we are headed.

Expect articles on market trends, operational excellence, property management insights, logistics innovation, and the broader economic landscape of Rwanda and East Africa. We look forward to building a knowledge-sharing community around the sectors that matter most to our business and our region.
